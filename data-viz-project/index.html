College Student State of Mind

A look at the reported mental health and wellbeing of college students across the country.

By Katherine Schutzman

April 19, 2026

The Healthy Minds Study aims to provide a detailed picture of mental health and related issues in college student populations. Each participating school provides the HMS team with a sample of currently enrolled students over the age of 18, either randomly selected or their entire student population. Schools with graduate students typically include both undergraduates and graduate students in the sample.
